Joining PwC was a natural next step after Chula. The consulting environment pushes you to think fast, communicate clearly, and deliver insights that actually move the needle.

Day to day, I work with data — cleaning it, analyzing it, turning it into stories that clients can act on. The tools change (SQL, Python, Power BI, Excel), but the core skill stays the same: figuring out what matters in a sea of numbers.

What I enjoy most is the variety. No two projects are the same, and every engagement teaches me something new about an industry, a process, or a way of thinking.
