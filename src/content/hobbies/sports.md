Badminton is my main sport. It's fast, technical, and surprisingly strategic — you're constantly reading your opponent and adjusting mid-rally.

I play regularly with friends and occasionally in local club matches. It's the best kind of exercise: competitive enough to push you, social enough to keep you coming back.

There's a satisfying parallel between badminton and analytical work. Both reward pattern recognition, quick decision-making, and staying calm under pressure.
