Photography is how I slow down. During the week, everything moves fast — deadlines, deliverables, data. On weekends, I pick up my camera and walk.

I'm drawn to street photography: the interplay of light and shadow, candid moments, the geometry of urban spaces. Bangkok is endlessly interesting for this — there's always something happening if you look closely enough.

I shoot mostly on a Fujifilm setup. The film simulations keep things simple and the colors feel right without heavy editing.
